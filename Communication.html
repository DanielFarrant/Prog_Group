Communication

in computing

Communication is probably the biggest and most widespread application of computer science. With advances in communication it is now possible to send messages around the world instantaneously, allowing faster dissemmination of information, allowing people to keep up to date with global news and events. Also the development of mobile phones allows people to keep in touch with friends and relatives over long distances.

Communication between different devices is also important, such as in Sat-Nav apps, like Google Maps, where the information-sharing between millions of devices is crucial to knowing and predicting traffic faster and more accurately than a traditional radio traffic announcement, to save people time on their daily commute to work.

In the future

The biggest advances in communication between different devices are in the area of cloud computing. The ability to upload, share and keep track of work in real-time between multiple locations will enable many people to work at home and save themselves the commute daily, in many cases saving people upwards of 2 hours a day in the week.
